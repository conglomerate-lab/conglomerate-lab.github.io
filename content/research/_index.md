Our group develops computational methods for coupled geomechanical systems, with emphasis on multiphase flow, heat transfer, and granular-fluid interactions.

    <h2>Coupled LBM–DEM Methods</h2>
    <p>We develop coupled lattice Boltzmann and discrete element methods to simulate interactions between fluids, particles, and porous materials.</p>
  </div>
</div>
