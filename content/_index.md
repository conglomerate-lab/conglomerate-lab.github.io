**Computation Geomechanics Research Team**

We develop computational methods for coupled geomechanical processes, with a focus on thermal, multiphase, and granular systems.

Our group is developing **LBMphaseX**, a coupled lattice Boltzmann method framework for thermal and multiphase flow, with coupling to the discrete element method.

We are part of the geotechnical engineering group at Virginia Tech.
